Goals

Short-Term

I would like to intern at a company that allows me to:

  • Exercise my creative muscles;
  • Take on various and complex challenges that have large scale impacts on user experience;
  • Continue to develop my communication and interpersonal skills;
  • Hone in on the techniques of creating solid and robust business models;
  • Learn more about the financial, marketing, and sales aspects of products.

Mid-Term

After graduation, my goal is to work at a company that:

  • Values my goal of impacting people's lives on a large scale;
  • Encourages creativity and openness of ideas;
  • Maintains a culture/atmosphere where everyday feels like an adventure.

I aspire to become a Product Manager, to be able to think of and improve upon existing solutions while working with those whose skill sets range from design, to business, to engineering.

Long-Term

My main reason for entering into the Computer Science field--besides the ingenuity and freedom to create--is to have the ability to find ways to solve real-world problems which are made efficient by technology. In this way, I can better understand the plausability of solutions which help users with their problems and needs as well as the work required to get to that point.

My aim is to solve extremely large-scaled problems using knowledge from the experiences and individuals I encountered throughout my career to advance the betterment of the world.
